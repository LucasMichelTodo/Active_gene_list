1 Actiev/Inactive Gene Lists

Our aim is to create a unified table that assigns to each gene in the P.falicparum gnome a expresion state. We will define 4 possible expresion states:

  • Active
    • Regular
    • Variant Active
    • Variant Repressed
  • Inactive
